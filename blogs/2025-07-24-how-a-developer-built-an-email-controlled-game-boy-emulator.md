        <h3>The Beautiful Chaos of Email Gaming</h3><p>PostmarkGBA has been running 24/7 in Rens' home office for over a month now, and it's accidentally become a social experience. Friends collaborate on strategies, share walkthroughs, and provide feedback - all through the humble medium of email.&nbsp;</p><p>The project perfectly captures the spirit of creative development: sometimes the best innovations come from asking "what if?" instead of "should I?" It's a reminder that the most memorable projects often emerge from developers who aren't afraid to be a little ridiculous and follow their curiosity wherever it leads.</p><p>Plus, it proves that <a href="https://postmarkapp.com/inbound-email">Postmark's Inbound processing</a> can handle pretty much anything you throw at it - even if what you're throwing is a request to make Pikachu use Thunder Shock.</p><p><i>Built something cool with Postmark lately?&nbsp;</i><a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1PbGaFctNBDr93rx0FWf6SDI0apkB36L-Wf8kLX_gDEQ/prefill"><i>Tell us about it</i></a></p>
