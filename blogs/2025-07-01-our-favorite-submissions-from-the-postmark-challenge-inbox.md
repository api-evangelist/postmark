When we launched the Postmark Challenge: Inbox Innovators on DEV Community, we expected clever solutions. What we got were projects that completely reimagined what email could do. Developers didn't just parse emails, they turned inboxes into gaming platforms, AI transcription services, environmental monitoring systems, podcast distribution channels, and automated code review systems.
